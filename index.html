Hey hun! We are Jamie and Dana of LBA, licensed acupuncturists in the state of Pennsylvania. We started treating patients together during our third year of graduate school at the Won Institute Acupuncture Clinic. We immediately shared a love for combining different styles of acupuncture and for integrating its endless wisdom into our lives. We wanted to keep working together, so we are!

LBA is here to serve the larger creation of access to deep care, and to bring ourselves and our community health and joy. We hope to see you on the table!

Jamie primarily practices Acupuncture Physical Medicine and loves treating musculoskeletal pain. She also loves treating women's health. She is currently enrolled in the Facial Applications for Cosmetic Enhancement certificate program through the Pacific College of Health and Sciences in New York and is excited to bring cosmetic acupuncture to LBA in 2024.
